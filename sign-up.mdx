Welcome! This guide will walk you through the process of registering on our platform, designed for the classification of real or artificially generated images.
<br/> Follow these steps to quickly access the service and start classifying videos and images.

## 1. Accessing the Platform
### Step 1: Visit the website
Open your preferred browser and type the platform's [URL](https://web.identifai.net) into the address bar.
### Step 2: Find the registration button
Once the page has loaded, you will find yourself in the login page, look for the Sign up button. The button is located at the top right of the login card.

## 2. Entering Personal Information
### Step 1: Fill in the registration form
Clicking the Register button will take you to a page with a registration form. Provide the following information in the required fields:
- **Name**: Enter the name you will use on the platform to personalize your experience.
- **Email Address**: Use a valid email address, as it will be required for account verification.
- **Password**: Create a secure password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase letters, numbers, and special symbols (e.g., *!@#$).
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to ensure there are no typos.
<Note>
  For signing up you need to accept the terms of service by ticking the relevant box.
</Note>
